Welcome Visitors!

Crusalis is a Geopolitical Roleplaying server founded by the 2 owners Rurik(hroerek) and Kermit(gaztons) in the summer of 2022! The Server lets thousands of players connect during war periods to play in large scale global conflicts

The Server chooses to run iterations which take place roughly every 2-3 months and last between 4-8 weeks in length. Throughout Crusalis History There Have Been the Following Iterations:

List of Iterations:

  • Age Of Discovery 1
  • Pappelonian War
  • Age Of Discovery 2
  • World War 1
  • Rise Of Rome
  • Sengoku Jidai
  • Iron Century
  • World War 1 v2
  • World War 2
  • Age of Discovery 3
