Crusalis is a Geopolitical Roleplaying server founded by the 2 owners Rurik(hroerek) and Kermit(gaztons) in the summer of 2022! The Server lets thousands of players connect during war periods to play in large scale global conflicts!

Visit the official Crusalis website for more info.
